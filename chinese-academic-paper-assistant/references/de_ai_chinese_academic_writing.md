# De-AI Chinese Academic Writing

## Goal

Make Chinese academic writing natural, concrete, and credible while preserving facts, citations, claims, and author intent. Do not promise AI-detector evasion.

## Remove Common AI-Flavored Patterns

Avoid or replace:

- `随着时代的发展`
- `在当今社会背景下`
- `具有重要意义`
- `不难看出`
- `综上所述可以看出`
- `首先、其次、最后` when the structure feels mechanical
- Repeated `通过...实现...`
- Overly balanced `不仅...而且...`

## Preferred Style

- Start from the research problem, evidence, or literature pattern.
- Use concrete nouns and verbs.
- Keep claims proportional to evidence.
- Vary sentence length.
- Let paragraphs have natural emphasis rather than perfect symmetry.
- Keep domain terms when useful; do not over-polish into vague prose.

## Revision Checklist

Before finalizing text:

- Are all citations preserved?
- Did any factual claim change?
- Is any claim too broad for the cited evidence?
- Are there hollow transitions or template endings?
- Does each paragraph move the review forward?

## Example Moves

Instead of:

```text
随着数字技术的不断发展，乡村治理具有重要意义。
```

Write:

```text
数字技术进入乡村治理后，基层治理的议题逐渐从资源投入转向主体协同、数据使用和公共服务响应能力。
```
