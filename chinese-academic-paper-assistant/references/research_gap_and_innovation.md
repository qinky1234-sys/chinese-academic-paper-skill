# Research Gap and Innovation

In one-click review mode, do not output research gaps or innovation points by default. Mention this module only if the user asks to continue after the complete literature review.

## Gap Categories

Check these categories:

- Research object: population, institution, industry, platform, region, or group.
- Scenario: policy stage, digital environment, rural/urban setting, platform ecology, emergency setting.
- Method: qualitative, quantitative, mixed methods, case comparison, network analysis, text mining, experiments.
- Data: newer data, micro data, longitudinal data, local data, platform data, interview data.
- Theory: missing mechanism, weak conceptual model, insufficient framework integration.
- Time period: old data, missing post-policy or post-event evidence.
- Mechanism path: unclear mediators, moderators, causal chain, or implementation process.
- Practice conversion: weak policy implications or operational recommendations.

## Innovation Point Format

Generate 3-5 options. Each option must include:

- 创新点名称
- 现有研究基础
- 尚未充分解决的问题
- 最接近论文：每个创新点列出 1-3 篇来自用户导入文献、已核验文献或公开来源候选文献的最接近论文
- 相近之处：说明这些论文与创新点在对象、场景、方法、理论、数据或结论上的接近点
- 可错开或推进的空间：说明用户如何避免只是重复已有论文
- 推荐方法论：案例研究、问卷调查、访谈、文本分析、实证模型、比较研究、政策文本分析等
- 用户可以怎么做
- 需要补充的数据或材料
- 适合写入论文的位置
- 适合论文类型：本科论文、硕士论文或期刊论文
- 风险与核验要求

## Closest Paper and Methodology Rules

- Use cautious wording such as `在当前文献范围内较接近` or `可作为相近研究参考`.
- If the closest paper comes from public-source candidate literature, mark it `待核验`.
- Do not claim that an innovation point is new only because no close paper was found in the current corpus.
- Do not fabricate closest papers, methods, sample sizes, data sources, or conclusions.
- Methodology suggestions are design references only; they do not replace adviser review or formal research design.

## Language Rules

Use cautious phrases:

- `在当前已检索文献范围内较少发现...`
- `已有研究更多集中于...`
- `仍有进一步讨论空间...`
- `该方向能否成立，需要扩大检索后确认...`
- `与该创新点最接近的研究可能是...，但仍需核验原文...`
- `该方向更像是在已有研究基础上的对象/方法/场景推进...`

Avoid:

- `没有人研究过`
- `填补国内外空白`
- `首次提出`
- `必然具有创新性`
- `已经证明该创新点成立`

## Feasibility Filter

Reject or downgrade innovation points that:

- Require data the user cannot plausibly obtain.
- Depend on unverified literature gaps.
- Are too broad to become one paper.
- Merely rename an existing topic.
- Promise causality without data or design.
